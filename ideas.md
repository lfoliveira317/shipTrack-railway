<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Swiss Style (International Typographic Style)**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Grid-Based Precision**: Strict adherence to a mathematical grid for layout, ensuring alignment and order.
  2.  **Objective Clarity**: Prioritizing legibility and objective presentation of data over decoration.
  3.  **Asymmetry**: Using asymmetric layouts to create dynamic tension and visual interest.
  4.  **Sans-Serif Typography**: Exclusive use of clean, grotesque sans-serif fonts (like Helvetica or Inter) for neutrality.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **High Contrast Monochrome**: Dominant black and white to emphasize structure.
  *   **Signal Orange**: A single, bold accent color (#FF5722) used sparingly for calls to action and critical alerts, representing the "Beacon" signal.
  *   **Intent**: To convey trust, reliability, and absolute precision in supply chain management.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Modular & Asymmetric**: Content is organized in distinct modules that don't always center-align. Large whitespace areas balance dense data tables.
  *   **Split Screens**: Vertical division of the screen to separate navigation/context from the active workspace.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Thick Divider Lines**: Bold horizontal rules separating sections.
  2.  **Oversized Typography**: Headlines that span the full width of the container.
  3.  **Geometric Icons**: Simple, solid shapes for iconography.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Immediate Feedback**: Hover states are sharp and instantaneous (no fade-ins).
  *   **Data-First**: Interactions focus on revealing more data (expanding rows, tooltips) without unnecessary transitions.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Snap & Slide**: Elements enter with quick, linear movements. No bounces or elastic effects.
  *   **Precise Reveals**: Dropdowns and modals appear instantly or with a very fast slide (150ms).

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Primary**: Inter (or system sans-serif) - Weights: 400 (Body), 600 (Headers), 800 (Display).
  *   **Hierarchy**: Massive headings (4rem+) contrasted with small, uppercase labels (0.75rem, tracking-wide).
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Glassmorphism / Neo-Futurism**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Translucency**: Using background blur and semi-transparent layers to create depth and context.
  2.  **Vivid Gradients**: Soft, glowing gradients in the background to suggest energy and flow.
  3.  **Floating Elements**: Cards and panels appear to float above the background, creating a 3D layered effect.
  4.  **Rounded Organic Shapes**: Moving away from sharp corners to softer, more fluid forms.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Deep Ocean Blue**: Dark, rich blues (#0A192F) as the base, representing the sea/logistics.
  *   **Neon Accents**: Cyan (#00E5FF) and Amber (#FFC107) for status indicators and highlights.
  *   **Intent**: To evoke a sense of modern, high-tech visibility and the fluid nature of global trade.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Card-Based Dashboard**: Everything is contained within "glass" cards.
  *   **Z-Axis Layering**: Important elements sit "higher" (lighter, more opaque) than secondary elements.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Frosted Glass Cards**: White/Dark backgrounds with low opacity and backdrop-filter: blur.
  2.  **Glowing Borders**: Subtle inner shadows or border gradients to define edges.
  3.  **Mesh Gradients**: Background orbs of color that shift slowly.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Fluidity**: Elements float and drift.
  *   **Soft Focus**: Hover states increase the "glow" or opacity of an element.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Float & Fade**: Elements drift into place with ease-out curves.
  *   **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background layers relative to foreground content.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Primary**: DM Sans or Poppins - Geometric but friendly.
  *   **Hierarchy**: Light weights for large headings, medium weights for body text to maintain readability against complex backgrounds.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Industrial Brutalism (Refined)**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Raw Materials**: Visuals that mimic paper, cardboard, or raw metal textures (subtly).
  2.  **Monospaced Data**: Heavy use of monospaced fonts for all data points, emphasizing the "logistics" aspect.
  3.  **Visible Structure**: Exposing the grid lines and borders rather than hiding them.
  4.  **High Functionality**: Density of information is a feature, not a bug.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Cardboard & Concrete**: Warm greys (#E5E5E5) and beige (#F0EBE0) backgrounds.
  *   **Shipping Container Colors**: Rust Red (#A52A2A), Safety Yellow (#F9A825), and Navy Blue (#1A237E) for categorization.
  *   **Intent**: To ground the digital tool in the physical reality of shipping and warehousing.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  *   **Dashboard Density**: Tightly packed grids with visible borders between every cell.
  *   **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent, tool-like sidebar that resembles a manifest.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Barcode Motifs**: Decorative elements resembling barcodes or shipping labels.
  2.  **Visible Borders**: 1px solid black borders on all cards and inputs.
  3.  **Tag-like Labels**: Status indicators that look like physical stickers.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  *   **Tactile**: Buttons have a "pressed" state that mimics physical buttons (3D transform).
  *   **Direct Manipulation**: Drag-and-drop interactions are emphasized.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Mechanical**: Transitions feel like mechanical switches or sliding doors.
  *   **No Blur**: Hard cuts and slides.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Primary**: Roboto (UI) + JetBrains Mono (Data).
  *   **Hierarchy**: Strong distinction between labels (uppercase, bold) and values (monospace).
</idea>
